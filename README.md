# 🛒 Blinkit Grocery Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Blinkit Grocery Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. This dashboard analyzes grocery sales data to provide meaningful insights into sales performance, customer ratings, outlet performance, and product categories.

The dashboard enables users to explore business data through dynamic filters, KPIs, and interactive visualizations, helping stakeholders make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze Blinkit's grocery sales performance.
- Monitor key business metrics using KPI cards.
- Identify top-performing products and outlets.
- Compare sales across outlet types, sizes, and locations.
- Enable interactive filtering for better business analysis.
- Support decision-making with meaningful visual insights.

---

## 💼 Business Problem Statement

Blinkit operates through multiple outlets and offers thousands of grocery products. As sales data grows, it becomes increasingly difficult to identify high-performing products, evaluate outlet performance, and understand customer preferences.

The business requires an interactive dashboard that consolidates sales information into a single view, allowing management to quickly monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make strategic business decisions.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

- What is the total sales generated?
- What is the average customer rating?
- Which item category contributes the highest sales?
- Which outlet location performs the best?
- Which outlet size generates maximum revenue?
- Which outlet identifier records the highest sales?
- How are sales distributed by item fat content?
- Which products have the highest visibility?
- How do different outlet types contribute to overall sales?
- What are the overall sales trends across different product categories?

## 📈 Key Insights

- Total Sales reached **56.95K**.
- Average Customer Rating is **4.56**.
- Total Ratings recorded are **2K**.
- Total Item Visibility is **27.97**.
- Snack Foods generated the highest sales.
- Tier 2 outlets performed better than other outlet locations.
- Medium-sized outlets contributed the highest revenue.
- Low Fat products generated more sales compared to Regular products.
- Outlet **OUT049** achieved the highest sales performance.
